WSU's general education assessment is guided by the Transparency Framework developed by the National Institute of Learning Outcomes Assessment. This framework seeks to make evidence of student accomplishment readily accessible, useful and meaningful to faculty, administration, students, and others. For more information, see About UCORE Assessment.
