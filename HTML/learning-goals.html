Student Learning Goals

All bachelor's degree requirements are rooted in WSU’s Seven Learning Goals and Outcomes, which identify core skills and knowledge that students should develop through their undergraduate studies (see below). Courses in the UCORE curriculum engage students in meeting these goals.

Alignment of Seven Learning Goals of the Baccalaureate and Learning Outcomes in Degree Programs

All WSU’s undergraduate degrees align their student learning outcomes with the Seven Goals of the Baccalaureate, with some variation based on disciplinary focus.

Additional Information

WSU’s Learning Goals of the Baccalaureate are developed by faculty and most recently updated in 2010. Additional information about general education and the Seven Learning Goals is available at the UCORE website and handbook
