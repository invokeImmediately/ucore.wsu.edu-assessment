Assessment Resources

Assessment resources encompass information or training provided to faculty and staff to help them understand, develop, implement, communicate, and use evidence of student learning.

The table below shows a variety of resources to support WSU faculty and programs assessing the Seven Learning Goals and UCORE outcomes, with links to contacts and materials.

Type of Resource Led By / Contact Description
UCORE Committee Faculty Chair, Vice Provost for Undergraduate Education UCORE Committee includes its sub-committee for assessment. Both committees include faculty and key administrative professionals under the Provost’s area. UCORE handbook (update in progress 2016)
Office of Assessment of Teaching and Learning (ATL) Kimberly Green

Assists with planning and supporting general education assessment, analyzing data and reporting/preparing results for discussion.

  • VALUE Rubrics - AAC&U and WSU adaptations (contact ATL)
  • Good practice samples from WSU programs (see news spotlights, contact ATL for other samples)
  • Capstone assignment workshop and resources (Hutchings)
  • ATL assessment toolkits address learning outcomes, assessment planning, methods, measures, and analysis, and using results
  • Consultations or trainings with programs and faculty (contact ATL)
